Alternative Methods of Injecting Stand

If the Stand Launchpad doesn't suit your needs, alternatives are available.

Automatic Injection via DLL

You can download our D3DCOMPILER_43.dll and place it in your game directory to have it automatically update and inject Stand when you start the game.

However, the Launchpad with its "Automatically inject when game starts" option should be a better solution all-around, because it allows you to launch the game, and it downloads Stand updates before launching the game, so early-inject enhancements will consistently work.

Using Another Injector

Stand's DLLs are entirely self-contained and therefore can be used with any injector, such as Xenos, but this option comes at the cost of auto-updating. You can still manually update when Stand gives you an update notification by unloading it and then injecting the new DLL.
